About Me

I am an extremely passionate and goal-oriented person with a burning desire to build and design air and spacecraft. On any day, I can be found watching the latest rocket launches, looking at flight patterns of the nearby airports, and catching up on the recent aerospace news. This love of all things aerospace led me to pursue an aerospace engineering degree at the University of Colorado at Boulder. While in pursuit of my degree, I have found a particular interest in aerodynamics, thermodynamics, and propulsion. Further, while in college, I have honed my C++ and MATLAB skills alongside expanding my economic knowledge as I pursue a double minor in computer science and economics. Outside of school I enjoy learning more about aerospace topics and gaining new skills on both the software and human side of things. I am currently in the process of learning python and I am continually expanding my knowledge of CAD software.
